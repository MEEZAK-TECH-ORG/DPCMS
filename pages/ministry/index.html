EVANGELICAL OUTREACH

Raising God Lovers, Building & Fulfilling Destinies

We are not a church but an interdenominational global community of God lovers, committed to empowering individuals through the gospel, helping them discover their purpose and fulfill their God-given destinies.

Our Vision

Empowering men to discover, pursue and fulfil their God-given destinies, igniting their passions and equipping them with the tools and resources needed to be all that God created them to be. We are not a church but, an interdenominational global community of God lovers. With a mandate to help men discover their significance and purpose here on earth, and empowering them to fulfilling it.

GEN. 1:28, COL.1:16, ACT 17:28, MATT 22:37-40

Our Mission

Raising men to nurture a deep and personal relationship with God through evangelical activities, helping them uncover their purposes and passion and equipping them with the skills, resources and empowerment for success. To achieve our goals by revealing God and His purpose to His people through preaching of the undiluted word of God and empowering them in discovering and fulfilling their destinies.

GEN. 1:28, COL.1:16, ACT 17:28, MATT.22:37-40

Our Goals & Objectives

Strategic initiatives designed to transform lives and build lasting community impact

Gospel Preaching

To preach the gospel of salvation to the world and guiding men to living a life that is inspired and controlled by the Lord.

Biblical Principles

By relying on biblical principles of love, generosity, stewardship and community, we strive to help men discover their destinies and earthly significance.

Community Impact

Building stronger communities through collaborative efforts, shared resources, and mutual support that empowers individuals and families.

Empowerment & Growth

Fostering personal and professional development through education, mentorship, and opportunities that unlock potential and create lasting change.

Financial Empowerment

Providing access to financial resources, savings programs, and investment opportunities that enable members to achieve economic independence and security.

Sustainable Development

Creating long-term solutions and programs that address systemic challenges while building resilience and sustainability within our communities.
