Daily thrift

Daily Savers Thrift is our daily savings empowerment program that encourages individuals to save a fixed amount of money daily.

31

Days to maturity

30%

Return on investment

N1,500

Registration fee

How it works

A simple, transparent process designed to help you build
saving habits.

1

Register and Empower

Complete your empowerment registration with a minimum of N1,500.00 to get started on your savings journey.

2

Set your Target

Determine how much you can comfortably save each day. Start small and build momentum over time.

3

Daily Deposits

Make your daily contributions to your designated thrift account. Consistency is key to success.

4

Track and Grow

Watch your savings grow daily. Your thrift matures at 31 days with attractive returns.

5

Receive Returns

After 30 days accrued period, receive your principal plus 30% returns and foodstuffs/household incentives.

6

Continue Building

Re-invest for 12 months and get empowered with your total accrued amount plus incentives.

Saving Tips

Maximize your thrift experience with these proven strategies.

Start Small, Think Big

Begin with a modest daily amount and gradually increase it over time as your income grows.

Automate Your Savings

Set up automatic transfers from your account to make saving effortless and prevent missed days.

Separate Accounts

Keep your thrift savings separate from everyday spending to avoid unnecessary temptation.

Regular Reviews

Monitor your progress regularly and adjust your daily amount to stay aligned with your financial goals.
