Semestral thrift

The Semestral Thrift is our top-tier investment opportunity designed to help you earn more and grow your wealth. Start saving today and enjoy great investment returns at the end of the 25 weeks to reach your target savings goals.

How it works

A simple, transparent process designed to help you build
consistent saving habits.

1

Register and Empower

Join the Semestral Thrift Empowerment program by registering with ₦2,000 and agreeing to the terms and conditions.

2

Weekly contributions

After registration fee of ₦2,000, contribute ₦1,500 weekly for a fixed period of 25 weeks.

3

Build Your Savings

Your contribution for 25 weeks totals ₦37,500, building a strong foundation for your goals.

4

Automatic Top-Up

After contributing for 25 weeks, receive a make-up of ₦37,500, bringing your total to ₦75,000.

5

Referral Rewards

Upon contributing 50 weeks worth ₦75,000 within 25 weeks of registration, refer at least 5 new active members to qualify as your downlines.

6

Empowerment Benefits

Your account qualifies for empowerment of ₦150,000 plus foodstuff/household items to support your livelihood.

7

Enhanced Rewards

If you refer at least 1 new active member within the first 25th weeks, receive ₦150,000 plus foodstuffs/household items after 50 weeks.

8

No-Referral Option

Without new member referrals, you'll still be empowered with ₦75,000 plus food product incentives after 50 weeks.

Semestral savings journey

What happens and when during your Semestral Thrift journey

Week 1: Registration

Pay ₦2,000 registration fee and agree to program terms. Begin your weekly ₦1,500 contributions.

Weeks 1-25: Active Contribution

Contribute ₦1,500. Start referring new members for bonus rewards.

Week 25: Automatic Top-Up

Receive ₦37,500 make-up amount, bringing your total thrift value to ₦75,000.

Weeks 26-50: Referral Period

Your thrift continues to mature. Focus on referring new members to unlock higher empowerment levels.

Week 50: Empowerment

Receive your empowerment based on referrals: ₦75,000 to ₦150,000 plus valuable household items and foodstuffs.
