How it works

A simple, transparent process designed to help you build consistent saving habits.

1

Register and Empower

Open your Semestral package with registration and activate your medium-cycle thrift plan.

2

Weekly Contributions

Save N1,500 weekly and maintain steady contributions through the 25-week cycle.

3

Build Your Savings

Grow your savings balance while staying disciplined and focused on your target.

4

Automate Top-Up

Use reminders or transfers to avoid missed weeks and improve completion confidence.

5

Referral Rewards

Meet referral goals during the cycle to remain qualified for package empowerment.

6

Empowerment Benefits

Complete the cycle and proceed to approved empowerment and support benefits.

Saving Tips

Maximize your thrift experience with these proven strategies.

Plan Monthly Targets

Break your 25-week goal into monthly checkpoints to stay motivated and consistent.

Save Before Expenses

Prioritize your weekly thrift contribution before discretionary spending each week.

Monitor Progress Often

Review your savings dashboard frequently and fix gaps early in the cycle.

Keep Referral Notes

Track referrals and support timelines so your benefit qualification stays on course.
