How it works

A simple, transparent process designed to help you build consistent saving habits.

1

Register and Empower

Complete registration and activate your Trimester Thrift with the required onboarding fee.

2

Start Weekly Savings

Contribute N1,500 weekly for 13 weeks to stay on track with your selected package plan.

3

Reach Milestone

Complete your first milestone window and maintain consistency for stronger results.

4

Automate Top-Up

Set automatic weekly top-ups to avoid missed contributions and build steady discipline.

5

Build Your Network

Refer members within your timeline to strengthen your qualification status and growth.

6

Account Tracking

Track your savings progress and package status from week one through maturity.

Saving Tips

Maximize your thrift experience with these proven strategies.

Stick to Your Weekly Plan

Treat your weekly contribution as a fixed bill so your savings momentum remains uninterrupted.

Save Early in the Week

Contribute early to avoid last-minute pressure and reduce the chance of missing a cycle.

Track Referral Deadlines

Set reminders for referral targets to meet package requirements within the expected window.

Review Progress Weekly

Check your balance growth every week and correct gaps before they affect completion.
