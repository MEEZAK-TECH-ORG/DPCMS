How it works

A simple, transparent process designed to help you build
consistent saving habits.

1

Register and Empower

Join the Trimestral Thrift Empowerment program by registering with ₦3,000.00 and agreeing to the terms and conditions.

2

Start Weekly Savings

After registration, contribute ₦1,500 weekly for a fixed period of 13 weeks.

3

Reach milestone

Your contribution for the thirteen (13) weeks totals ₦19,500.

4

Automatic Top-Up

To automatically make your Thrift mature for empowerment after 13 weeks, you will be required to pay a make-up of 37 weeks thrift i.e. amount to ₦55,500.00, making your total contribution ₦75,000.00, which is the value of the Fifty(50) weeks matured Thrift.

5

Build Your Network

At the end of the trimester, you receive your total accumulated savings, ready to be used for planned goals or personal needs.

6

Account Tracking

Reinvest for 12 months and get empowered with your total accrued amount plus incentives.

7

Premium Empowerment

To automatically make your Thrift mature for empowerment after 13 weeks, you will be required to pay a make-up of 37 weeks thrift i.e. amount to ₦55,500.00, making your total contribution ₦75,000.00, which is the value of the Fifty(50) weeks matured Thrift.

8

Alternative Outcome

At the end of the trimester, you receive your total accumulated savings, ready to be used for planned goals or personal needs.

9

Referral/No referral option

Upon contributing 50 weeks worth ₦75,000 within 25 weeks of registration, refer at least 5 new active members to qualify as your downlines. Upon no referral of new member, you shall be empowered with your contributed amount of ₦75,000 and food product incentive after 50 weeks.

Trimestral savings journey

What happens and when during your Trimestral Thrift journey

Week 1: Registration

Pay ₦3,000 registration fee and begin your weekly ₦1,500 contributions for 13 weeks.

Weeks 1-13: Active Contribution

Contribute ₦1,500 every week. Total contributions reach ₦19,500, and actively refer new members. This is your key growth period.

Week 13: Make-Up Payment

Pay ₦55,500 make-up amount to bring your total to ₦75,000 (equivalent to 50 weeks of thrift).

Fast Track: With 10+ Referrals

Receive immediate empowerment of ₦150,000 plus premium household incentives right after week 13!

Week 13: Empowerment

Receive your empowerment based on referrals: ₦75,000 to ₦150,000 plus valuable household items and foodstuffs.
