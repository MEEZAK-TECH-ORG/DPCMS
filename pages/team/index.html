OUR TEAM

The Team Behind the Vision

A dedicated team working together to empower individuals and strengthen our community through service, collaboration, and shared purpose.

Pst. Stephanie A. Augustine

President/CEO

Pst. Stephanie A. Augustine is a servant of God, visionary leader, and empowerment coach devoted to leading people to God and empowering them to live purposeful and fulfilling lives. She is the Lead Pastor of Destiny Promoters Evangelical Outreach Lagos, an interdenominational ministry focused on helping people discover and fulfill their destinies through evangelism and empowerment programs.


Driven by her compassion for humanity, she also founded Destiny Promoters Cooperative, the empowerment arm of the ministry, which promotes improved livelihoods through collaborative savings and community support. She provides pastoral care, teaches God’s word, operates in the prophetic, and supports individuals in taking ownership of their faith and lives.


She holds a B.Sc. in Microbiology, certifications in Strategic Management and Christian Ministry, a Master’s in Theological Studies, and is currently pursuing postgraduate studies in Christian Ministry and Leadership. Her mission is to raise people to be earthly significant and heavenly useful.
