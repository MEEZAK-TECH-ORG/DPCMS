What is DPCMS?

DPCMS is a christian non-governmental empowerment organization, an affiliates of Destiny Promoters Evangelical Outreach (DPEO).

DPEO helps people discover and fulfill their God-given purpose through Christian outreach. Our cooperative serves as the empowerment arm.

DPEO is duly registered with CAC Nigeria (RC:7168250) and DPCMS Lagos State Ministry of Commerce, cooperative, trade and investment (LSCS:19247)

Empowering individuals and communities toward financial stability, better livelihoods, and fulfilling lives through collaborative contribution.

Our Vision

Empowering individuals and communities to break free from the life of poverty and dependence, and to have access to financial stability, abundant food, safe and comfortable housing, high quality education and skills training for self-growth and development.

Our Mission

Equipping individuals and communities to thrive in a life of self-sufficiency, dignity and purpose by creating a collaborative community where combined resources are harnessed to drive meaningful changes, enhance livelihood, build a brighter future and support kingdom-focused initiatives.

Our Goals & Objectives

Strategic initiatives designed to transform lives and build lasting community impact

Gospel Preaching

To preach the gospel of salvation to the world and guiding men to living a life that is inspired and controlled by the Lord. We are mandated to preach the undiluted word of God, helping men to discover their destinies and supporting them to fulfilling it.

Biblical Principles

By relying on biblical principles of love, generosity, stewardship and community, we strive to help men discover their destinies and earthly significance.

Community Impact

Building stronger communities through collaborative efforts, shared resources, and mutual support that empowers individuals and families.

Empowerment & Growth

Fostering personal and professional development through education, mentorship, and opportunities that unlock potential and create lasting change.

Financial Empowerment

Providing access to financial resources through collaborative financial contributions in form of thrift that enable members to achieve economic independence and security.

Sustainable Development

Creating long-term solutions and programs that address systemic challenges while building resilience and sustainability within our communities.

Kingdom Impact

To use our collective resources to support kingdom-focused initiatives, spreading the love of christ, transforming lives and expanding God's kingdom.
