Who we are

Destiny Promoters Cooperative (Onigbongbo) Multipurpose Society Lagos is a Christian non-governmental empowerment organization and the empowerment arm of Destiny Promoters Evangelical Outreach (DPEO), an interdenominational outreach focused on helping people discover and fulfill their purpose through evangelical and empowerment activities. The organization is duly registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria and the Lagos State Ministry of Commerce, Cooperative, Trade and Investment. It equips and empowers individuals and communities to achieve financial stability, improved livelihoods, and a fulfilling life through collaborative savings and community support.
