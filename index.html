Who we are

Destiny Promoters Cooperative (Onigbongbo) Multipurpose Society Lagos is a Christian non-governmental empowerment organization and the empowerment arm of Destiny Promoters Evangelical Outreach (DPEO), an interdenominational outreach focused on helping people discover and fulfill their purpose through evangelical and empowerment activities. The organization is duly registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria and the Lagos State Ministry of Commerce, Cooperative, Trade and Investment. It equips and empowers individuals and communities to achieve financial stability, improved livelihoods, and a fulfilling life through collaborative savings and community support.

How it works

Destiny Promoters Cooperative is a christian non-governmental empowerment organization that provides a secure and accessible savings platform through a culture of thrift.

1

To become a member, a non-refundable empowerment registration fee of three thousand naira (N2,000.00) is required.

2

After registration, contribute one thousand five hundred naira (N1,500.00) weekly as a member, for fifty (50) or twenty-five (25) or thirteen (13) weeks depending on your choice of plan.

3

After contributing a total of ₦75,000 over 50 weeks, you will be empowered with ₦150,000 and foodstuff incentives to help strengthen your livelihood.

4

Members are required to refer at least one person within their first 25 weeks to qualify for the cash and foodstuff incentives.
